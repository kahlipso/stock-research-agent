# Quality factor

Quality has 60% minimum weighted coverage. Profitability includes gross, operating, net and FCF margins; ROA, ROE, approximate ROIC (`NOPAT / (debt + equity - cash)`, with NOPAT approximated as EBIT × 0.79); and gross profit/assets. Cash quality includes OCF/net income, FCF/net income and `(net income - OCF)/assets`. Balance-sheet inputs include net debt/EBITDA, debt/equity and interest coverage. Stability/dilution includes three-period dispersion and one-/three-year diluted-share change.

Debt, EV, EBITDA, ROIC and interest-coverage features are excluded for banks, insurers, capital markets, mortgage finance and financial services. Invalid denominators remain null and reduce confidence.
