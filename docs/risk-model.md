# Risk model

Risk is separate from alpha. `0` means the lowest measured market risk and `100` the highest. Stored metrics include 20/60/252-observation annualized volatility, downside volatility, SPY beta/correlation when benchmark data exist, one-/three-year maximum drawdown, ATR percentage, largest gap, recent 5% gap frequency, idiosyncratic volatility where available and missing-bar percentage where a calendar is configured. Bands are Low (<25), Moderate (<50), High (<75), and Very high. Historical volatility is not business risk.
